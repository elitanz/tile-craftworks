# Tile Craftworks — Brainerd Lakes, MN

A custom website for **Tile Craftworks, LLC** — owner Brett Dean's hand-set tile
business serving the Brainerd Lakes area (Nisswa · Pequot Lakes · Brainerd ·
Crosslake). Custom showers, backsplashes, tile floors, and kitchen/bath remodels.
25 years in the trade, A+ rated by the BBB.

**Live:** https://elitanz.github.io/tile-craftworks/
**Call/text Brett:** (612) 723-6627

Static HTML/CSS/JS — no build step. Warm artisan dark theme (Fraunces + Hanken
Grotesk), glazed-tile mosaic signature band, image hero with scroll parallax,
pinned scroll-telling process, a drag-able **before/after slider**, lead form, and
a sticky mobile call bar.

> Photos are high-quality stock placeholders to be swapped with Brett's real
> project photos — also the natural sales hook.
