About Tile Craftworks

A Lakes-Area Tile
Craftsman

Tile Craftworks is owner Brett Dean's shop — a small, hands-on tile business serving the Brainerd Lakes area since 2016, backed by 25 years in the trade. Every shower, floor, and backsplash is set by hand, with the patience and precision that tile demands.

From a simple kitchen backsplash to a fully reimagined bathroom, the goal is the same: clean lines, lasting work, and a finish you'll be proud of for decades. A+ rated by the BBB, and built one careful job at a time.
